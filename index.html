FAQ

What is vetements?
vetements is an advanced all-in-one Discord bot designed to provide powerful utilities directly in your DMs and servers. No need to download external tools or visit multiple websites.

How do I use the bot?
Simply add vetements to your server or send it a DM, then use slash commands like /qrgen, /translate, or /cryptoprice to access various features.

Is vetements free?
Yes! Most features are completely free. Premium features unlock additional capabilities.
